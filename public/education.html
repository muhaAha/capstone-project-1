Applyed Faculty

      Instead of going on to medical faculty, I have decided to enroll in mechanical engineering. On year 1999th I have enrolled engineering studies at "University St. Cyril and Methodius" in Skopje. I had a great first year od studies. But in 2000th and 2001th year civil war broke out. After that, it was very difical to study as muslim and non-macedonian. I graduated here, but I was 7 examps back, so I didn`t have got a digree. There were nationalist and chauvinist teachers, especially form Bitola and Prilep cityes, who which hindered my further progress in my studies. My talents were at stake!
      So because of that situation, with 7 exams to pass, I have left the faculty and in 2011th year I moved to University of Tetova. After some years, I have finished successfully my studies there.
      What is imoptant for me to mention is that I had interest in Java programming language 3D modeling and Linux command shell (bash) before I went to Tetovo. At that time I had great skills in those areas.
      At Faculty of Applied Science in UNITE I earned my degree as a bachelor of science mechatronics engineer. I am proud of the great teachers, the modern teaching methods and the european academical environment there. On this occasion greet the vice-chancellor Hazir Polozhani, really great person and humanist.
